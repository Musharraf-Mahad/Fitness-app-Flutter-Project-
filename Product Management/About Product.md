# FitTrack – Product Vision

## 1. Product Overview

**FitTrack** is a mobile fitness tracking application designed to help users monitor their daily physical activity, track workouts, and achieve personal fitness goals. The application provides an intuitive interface that allows users to view their step count, monitor calories burned, track workout sessions, and manage fitness goals in a simple and motivating way.

The goal of FitTrack is to encourage users to adopt a healthier lifestyle by providing clear activity insights and easy-to-use tracking tools.

---

## 2. Product Vision

Our vision is to create a simple and accessible fitness tracking application that motivates users to stay active and maintain healthy habits through clear progress tracking and goal setting.

FitTrack aims to make fitness monitoring easy, visually engaging, and useful for everyday users who want to improve their physical activity levels.

---

## 3. Target Users

FitTrack is designed for:

* Individuals who want to track their daily steps
* Users beginning their fitness journey
* People who want to monitor workouts and physical activity
* Anyone who wants to set and track fitness goals

---

## 4. Core Features

### User Authentication

* User login
* User registration
* Secure account management

### Dashboard

* Overview of daily fitness statistics
* Steps taken today
* Calories burned
* Distance covered
* Quick access to workouts

### Step Tracker

* Daily step count
* Progress toward daily step goal
* Activity visualization

### Workout Tracking

* Start workout sessions
* Track workout duration
* Monitor workout activity

### Goal Management

* Set daily step goals
* Track progress toward goals
* Visual goal completion indicators

### Profile Management

* View and edit user profile
* Personal information management
* Account settings
* Logout functionality

---

## 5. Key Value Proposition

FitTrack provides a simple and motivating way for users to monitor their daily activity and stay committed to their fitness goals without the complexity of advanced fitness platforms.

---

## 6. Success Metrics

The success of FitTrack will be measured through:

* User engagement with step tracking
* Completion of daily fitness goals
* Consistent workout tracking
* User satisfaction with the application interface

---

## 7. Future Improvements

Potential future enhancements include:

* Integration with wearable devices
* Social fitness challenges
* Advanced workout analytics
* Personalized fitness recommendations

---

## 8. Product Scope

This project focuses on delivering a functional mobile application with essential fitness tracking capabilities including activity monitoring, workout tracking, and goal management.

The application is developed as part of an academic mobile application project.
